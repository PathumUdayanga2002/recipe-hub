Chocolate Lava Cake

Indulge in these decadent individual chocolate cakes with a warm, gooey center that flows out when cut into. Perfect for chocolate lovers and impressive enough for dinner parties.

45 mins
Prep & Cook
4
Servings
Medium
Difficulty
Chocolate Baking Vegetarian Special Occasion

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 ounces high-quality semi-sweet chocolate
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • Butter for ramekins
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  • Powdered sugar for dusting
  • Vanilla ice cream for serving

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Butter 4 (6-ounce) ramekins and dust with cocoa powder.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine butter and chocolate. Microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each, until melted and smooth. Set aside to cool slightly.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, egg yolks, and sugar until pale and thick, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Gently fold the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture until combined.
  5. Sift flour and salt over the chocolate mixture and fold gently until just combined.
  6. Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins, filling about 3/4 full.
  7. Place ramekins on a baking sheet and bake for 12-14 minutes, until the edges are firm but the centers are soft.
  8. Remove from oven and let rest for 1 minute. Run a knife around the edges to loosen.
  9. Invert each ramekin onto a serving plate and carefully lift to release the cake.
  10. Dust with powdered sugar and serve immediately with vanilla ice cream.

Berry Parfait

A light and refreshing no-bake dessert with layers of creamy yogurt, crunchy granola, and fresh mixed berries. Perfect for a healthy treat or quick dessert that comes together in minutes.

20 mins
Prep Time
2
Servings
Easy
Difficulty
Fruit No-Bake Healthy Quick & Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 2 tablespoons chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pecans)
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Wash and prepare the berries. If using strawberries, hull and slice them.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the Greek yogurt, honey (or maple syrup), and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Take two serving glasses or bowls and start layering the ingredients.
  4. Begin with a layer of the yogurt mixture at the bottom of each glass.
  5. Add a layer of mixed berries on top of the yogurt.
  6. Sprinkle a layer of granola over the berries.
  7. Repeat the layers until the glasses are filled, ending with a yogurt layer on top.
  8. Sprinkle the chopped nuts over the top of each parfait.
  9. Garnish with a few fresh berries and mint leaves if desired.
  10. Serve immediately or refrigerate for up to 2 hours before serving.
